Maximize Your Yield

CropGuard Pro utilizes a unique dual-action formula that stops fungal infections on contact while also providing systemic protection from within the plant tissue. This ensures that new growth is protected even after application.

Ideal for use during the humid season when fungal spores are most active. Compatible with most common insecticides and fertilizers, allowing for easy tank mixing.

Key Benefits

  • Controls Powdery Mildew, Black Spot, and Rust.
  • Rainfast within 2 hours of application.
  • Low toxicity to beneficial insects like bees when dry.
  • Economical 500ml concentrate makes up to 100 liters of spray solution.
